Our Story

Founded in 2020, Edu Smartspark began as a small initiative to help university students access quality educational resources. What started as a YouTube channel sharing question papers has now grown into a comprehensive educational platform serving thousands of students across India.

Our founder, a former university professor, recognized the challenges students face in accessing reliable study materials and set out to create a solution that would bridge this gap.
